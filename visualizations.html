Word Cloud

The word cloud describes the themes and major messages that we have found to be commonly used within the student interviews. These words are directly from our data set and represent the values and harships of a diverse group of engineers.

Stacked Bar Plot

By collecting the key terms used by each individual during their interview, we are able to get a general sense of the common values and areas of importance to individuals in marginalized communities.

Process Flow

Our process flow chart depicts the ways in which the research project is conducted. By interviewing individuals of often underserved communities and analyzing the trends seen in these interviews, we can present these results to hopefully create positive change within the OSU engineering community and beyond.
